About

Objective of this hackathon is to nurture FinTech talent to meet the rapidly changing needs of the financial services sector. We invite students from the 5 local polytechnics to develop Fintech prototypes using Open APIs from the industry to address real world problem statements, such as more efficient GST refunds for tourists, AI-based e-payment service for hawker centres, peer-to-peer micro-lending platform for students, etc. Finalist will get a chance to showcase their ideas at the Singapore Fintech Festival 2021.

Registration Process

Step 1: Form a team

Form a team of 2 to 6 students of the same polytechnic and appoint a team leader.

Step 2: Register via online Google Form

Click here to register by 16 April 2021.

Step 3: Receive registration confirmation

Receive the registration confirmation from the organising committee by 30 April 2021.

Step 4: Attend online workshops

Once all your team members are registered, you are required to attend the online industry workshop(s) of the allocated category.

Online Industry Workshops

Every team has been allocated a category to compete in. All team leaders and members are to attend the online industry workshop(s) of the allocated category. Registration of the industry workshops is automatically done based on the category. (NOTE: There will be attendance taking before and after the online workshop)

The online meeting instruction will be sent one week before the scheduled workshop, to your email addresses used in the registration form. All teams are to attend the APIX workshop.
