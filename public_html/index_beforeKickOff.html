About

Objective of this hackathon is to nurture FinTech talent to meet the rapidly changing needs of the financial services sector. We invite students from the 5 local polytechnics to develop Fintech prototypes using Open Technologies from the industry to address real world problem statements, such as more efficient GST refunds for tourists, AI-based e-payment service for hawker centres, peer-to-peer micro-lending platform for students, etc. Finalist will get a chance to showcase their ideas at the Singapore Fintech Festival 2019.

Registration

Current students from 5 polytechnics are eligible to participate this hackathon.

Step 1: Form a team

Form a team of students of the same polytechnic and appoint a team leader.

Step 2: Decide a category to compete

Your team is to choose one of the 5 hackathon categories to compete.

Step 3: Register online

Only team leader is to register online using a valid polytechnic student email in Eventbrite. (Click here to register)

Step 4: Sign up for the Kick-off Event

All members in the team to sign-up and attend the kick-off event on 13th March at Temasek Polytechnic.(Click here to sign-up)

Step 5: Polytechnic representative to contact you.

Polytechnic staff representative of Hackathon 2019 will contact you via email or phone call to fill up your team members personal details.

Step 6: Attend workshops

Once all your team members are registered, sign up for the workshop for training by our sponsors.
